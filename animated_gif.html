The Perfect Day

by Rei Li

Animation & Design  ·  Rei Li

About This Animation

I made this one by mixing hand-drawn sketches with some Photoshop work — cutting, layering, and piecing things together frame by frame. The story is pretty simple: me and my golden retriever having one of those really good days at the park. Running around, goofing off, the usual.

I wanted it to feel personal rather than polished, so I kept some of the rougher hand-drawn lines instead of cleaning everything up. The name says it all, honestly — it's just a happy day with a dog I love a lot.
